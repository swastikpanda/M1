Mission

The mission of this web site is to recruit new members for the Townsville Community Music Centre . We also increase the engagement of members through our website. We can increase the profit of Townsville Community Music Centre by saleing the tickets though our website.

Gole

The gole of our website is to increase the ticket sale especially to the general public. We also recruit new members for our Community and also increase the engagement of new members in our activity.

Success Evaluation

The success of this webpage will depend on the following:

  • Increased number of volunteers for the Townsville Community Music Centre through our website
  • The number of New sponsors acquired through our website.
  • Increased nummber of members through our website.
  • Increased number of ticket sales though our website
  • How many new concrets were arrenged for new perfomers throug our website
